NavigatAR Guide

Our app ExplorAR will help you reach any IIITD building! Make sure you follow the following steps in order to use ExplorAR: 1) Ensure that you have an internet connection. 2) Ensure that your GPS is turned on. 3) Please allow access to your location when prompted. 4) When your camera opens up, you are ready to use ExplorAR! The default screen is Explore, where you can see pointers for all the buildings. 5) You can search for a specific building by clicking on the Search icon (magnifier), choose which building you want to go to from the menu. Once you choose your destination, ExplorAR tells you the distance(in metres) between you and your destination. Note: Distance shown may not be 100% accurate because of GPS instability.
